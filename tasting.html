A Culinary Journey

Our Chef's Tasting Menu is a carefully curated experience that takes you through the seasons and regions of Belgium and France. Each course tells a story, celebrating the finest ingredients and traditional techniques with modern flair.

01

Amuse-Bouche

Goat's cheese mousse with honeycomb, fresh herbs, and edible flowers. A delicate start to awaken the palate.

Wine Pairing: Champagne Brut Premier
02

Scallop Carpaccio

Hand-dived scallops, lemon zest, truffle oil, micro herbs, and sea salt. A celebration of the ocean's purity.

Wine Pairing: Chablis Premier Cru
03

Truffle Velouté

Black truffle essence, wild mushroom duxelles, and chive oil. Earthy elegance in every spoonful.

Wine Pairing: Meursault
04

Seared Duck Breast

Crispy duck breast, orange gastrique, confit shallots, and seasonal vegetables. A classic reimagined.

Wine Pairing: Pinot Noir, Burgundy
05

Fromage Course

Selection of French and Belgian cheeses with fig compote, walnut bread, and honey from Provence.

Wine Pairing: Sauternes
06

White Chocolate Parfait

White chocolate parfait, raspberry coulis, mint essence, and gold leaf. A symphony of sweetness.

Wine Pairing: Muscat de Beaumes-de-Venise
07

Petits Fours

Selection of handmade chocolates, Belgian macarons, and canelés to conclude your journey.

Pairing: Coffee or Tea Selection
